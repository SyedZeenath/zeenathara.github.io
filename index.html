About Me

I didn’t pivot away from software — I evolved through it. Seven years building industrial-grade systems, real-time dashboards, and AI reasoning engines taught me one thing: the most fascinating frontier isn’t on a screen. It’s in machines that sense, decide, and move.

Today I’m pursuing an MSc in Robotics & Embedded AI at Maynooth University, channelling that software intuition into ROS2, computer vision, and intelligent manipulation — applying the same engineering rigour I honed at Kongsberg Digital to robots that operate in the real world.

I’m a Women Techmakers Ambassador, a MUSE Platinum Award recipient, a GenAI Hackathon winner, and someone who believes the best robots are built by engineers who deeply understand both the code and the machine.
